about us

We are working since 2015 for food,water,education , and other

At [Shree Ram Chandra Manav Kalyan Samiti], we have been dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged individuals since our establishment in 2015. Our primary focus is on addressing the basic needs of food, water, and education for beggars and marginalized communities.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to create a more equitable society by providing essential support to those who are most vulnerable and often overlooked. We believe that every individual, regardless of their circumstances, deserves access to fundamental human rights such as food, clean water, and education.

Recent Blogs

Work has been started on the plan to make Kushinagar district a self-dependent beggar.

26 December 2022

we have embarked on a transformative journey to make Kushinagar district a self-dependent community, free from the need for begging as a means of survival. We believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to lead a dignified life and that by addressing the root causes of begging, we can empower the residents of Kushinagar to become self-sufficient and economically independent. , Our vision is to create a society in Kushinagar where every individual has access to education, employment, and support systems that enable them to break free from the cycle of begging. We strive to build a community that thrives on self-reliance, resilience, and inclusivity.

Mass marriage of poor girls in Kushinagar district

26 December 2022

Group marriage ceremonies for poor girls in Kushinagar district are a wonderful initiative that aims to provide financial assistance and support to underprivileged girls in their journey towards marriage. These ceremonies involve multiple couples getting married simultaneously, thereby reducing the financial burden on individual families. Group marriage ceremonies for poor girls in Kushinagar district not only contribute to the financial stability of families but also promote a sense of community and inclusivity. By coming together and supporting these initiatives, society can help create a more equitable environment for those in need.
